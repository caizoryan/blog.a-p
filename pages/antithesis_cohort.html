Anti-Thesis Cohort?

I will add more details here later... but for now...

Independent Workshop is a nomadic, collaboratively organized “thesis” cohort for those of us that are not taking the official class; it began as a solution to work around the current conditions of this year’s over-crowded thesis cohorts.

We work together to create a system of accountability that we would otherwise lack, and in the process, we run our own workshops, lock-in sessions, presentations, and general shenanigans.

We host guest Crits to get external feedback from people and hosted 3 of them til now.
